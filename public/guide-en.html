AI Model Setup Guide

3 simple steps to get local AI running on your machine — completely free, your data never leaves your computer.

Step 1
Install Ollama

Ollama is the program that lets AI models run directly on your PC — no cloud, no subscription.

Go to ollama.com and download the installer for Windows or Mac. Install it like any normal program (Next → Next → Finish).

Once installed, Ollama runs quietly in the system tray. You don't need to do anything in Ollama's UI itself.

Step 2
Choose the Right Model for Your PC

Check your RAM: Settings → System → About on Windows.

Model RAM Needed Speed Vietnamese Best For
qwen3.5:3b 4 GB ⚡ Very fast ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ ✓ 8 GB RAM PCs
llama3.2:3b 4 GB ⚡ Fast ⭐⭐⭐ ✓ 8 GB RAM PCs
qwen3.5:7b 8 GB 🟡 Moderate ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ ✓ 16 GB RAM PCs
phi-4 14 GB 🐢 Slow ⭐⭐⭐⭐ Needs 24 GB+ RAM
💡 Tip: Regular office PCs (8 GB RAM) → use qwen3.5:3b. 16 GB RAM → use qwen3.5:7b for better quality. Even small models work very well for structured financial news extraction.
Step 3
Download the Model

Open Command Prompt (press Win + R, type cmd, press Enter) and run:

For 8 GB RAM PCs (Best Choice):

ollama pull qwen3.5:3b

For 16 GB RAM PCs (Higher Quality):

ollama pull qwen3.5:7b

⏳ Download takes 2–10 minutes depending on your internet speed. Only needs to be done once.

When done, you'll see: success

✅ All done! Reopen MarketSense VN, go to Settings → AI Model, select the model you just downloaded, and click Save. Then hit Scout Now to start monitoring the market!

Need help? Visit ollama.com/library for the full list of available models.
